    About GoMerit Education

    GoMerit Education was established as an open and nonprofit resource for education system. It was put together by small group of educators and now the whole management runs under Ashish Patrick sir.

    Our aim is to provide a space for students to explore education, learn social action and in particularly the theory and practice of informal education, community learning and development and lifelong learning.

    As the New generation is well equipped with technologies we try to connect with them on their level for their better understanding. We have researched on this method of education and realized this helps in long term learning and making career of students.

    It’s an old saying “You can’t build a great building on a weak foundation. You must have a solid foundation if you’re going to have a strong superstructure”. Gordon B. Hinckley

    We concentrate on interactive sessions where children don’t get bored but instead take interest in topics. Our lecture includes visuals and videos which help students to memories and remember the topics. The optic nerves are the thickest nerve in human body that is the reason visuals are important while we teach the students.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is making education fun, easy and accessible Courses accommodated with complete study package along with short, fun and easy to understand videos. We help students to easily understand and master the subject and achieve their goals with flying colors.


    Video lectures for better grades

    Videos are fun fast and easy way for students to crack exams and understand concept better.


    Advantage of old method

    White board and colorful marker method for slow and deeper understanding.


    Assignments

    With assignments totally based on CBSE/NCERT pattern helps student to gain thorough knowledge of how exam comes.


    Old and new technology mixed culture

    New technology like videos animations video test assessment along with the advantages of old whiteboard and marker method helps students to understand and grasp concept more firmly.


    Student improvement

    Students have started saying how they like combination of videos and orthodox teaching method in class. Results of student have drastically improved.
